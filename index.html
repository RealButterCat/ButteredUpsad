About

ButteredUpsad is an experimental project that blends website design with game mechanics.

Once you activate the game, you can interact with ANY element on this page.

  • Click objects to inspect or attack them
  • Drag elements to move them
  • Collect items into your inventory
  • Break through walls and barriers
  • Talk to NPCs and complete quests
