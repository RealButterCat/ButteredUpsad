Welcome to ButteredUpsad

This seems like a normal website, but it hides a secret interactive RPG. Everything you see can be clicked, dragged, or broken!

ButteredUpsad is an experimental project that transforms a seemingly normal website into an interactive playground. Once you activate the game mode, you can interact with ANY element on this page.

Quick Tips:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Click on objects to interact with them
  • Press Tab to open your inventory
  • Try breaking walls, talking to NPCs, and collecting items
